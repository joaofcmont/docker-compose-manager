1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Docker Compose Manager, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: If you sign up for our waitlist or contact us, we collect your name, email address, and role.
  • Docker Compose Files: When you upload a Docker Compose YAML file, the file content is processed entirely in your browser. We do not store or transmit your Docker Compose files to our servers.
  • Survey Responses: If you complete our survey, we collect your feedback and responses to help improve our service.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, including:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage (e.g., file uploads, form submissions)
  • Device and browser information
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our service
  • Send you updates about Docker Compose Manager (if you've signed up for the waitlist)
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Docker Compose Files

Important: All Docker Compose file processing happens locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Store your Docker Compose files on our servers
  • Transmit your files over the network (except when you explicitly download them)
  • Have access to the contents of your files

Your files remain private and are never shared with us or third parties.

3.2 Other Data

Contact information and survey responses are stored securely using Firebase Firestore. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage (Firestore) and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS: For sending contact form emails. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: For hosting our application. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
